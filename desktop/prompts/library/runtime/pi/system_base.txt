Subjects library schema:
- `subjects/catalog.json`: 主体索引
- `subjects/categories.json`: 自定义分类列表
- `subjects/<subject-id>/subject.json`: 主体详情
- `subject.json` fields: `id`, `name`, `categoryId`, `description`, `tags`, `attributes`, `imagePaths`, `createdAt`, `updatedAt`
- `attributes` is a key-value list
- `imagePaths` are relative paths under the subject folder, usually in `images/`
